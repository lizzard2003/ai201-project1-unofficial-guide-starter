 Be cautious if you are considering signing a lease on a complex that is not completely built.
LOOK AT FIRE SAFETY Questions to Ask Yourself
• Does your dwelling have sprinklers and fire extinguishers?
• Do you have a fire escape plan?
• If you have pets or babies, is every room baby/pet proof?
Useful Tips
• Make sure that all smoke alarms are properly working.
• Test smoke alarms every month. If they are not working, CHANGE THE BATTERIES.
• Have a fire escape plan. (You may have as little as 2 minutes to escape.)
• Never smoke inside of your apartment/house (Go outside to smoke.)
• Invest in flameless candles.
• Cook on low temperatures.
• You will want to make sure a property is move-
in ready for the day you want and before school starts.
